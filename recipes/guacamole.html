Guacamole

Guacamole recipe

An avocado-based dip pefect for dunking, spreading and dipping

Intense flavour loaded with fibre and full of monosaturated fat

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 1 lime - juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados
  2. Add lime juice and salt
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper
  4. Refrigerate 1 hour
  5. Serve
